A little about me, Alvin and what we can do for you

My name's Jules Webb and I'm a web developer

I’m located in Washington state, but I work on projects from all over the US. I create modern, interactive, responsive, beautiful websites for my clients. I help them build an online presence that boosts their visibility on the web.

I built my first website in 2002 when my late husband and I started a 501(c)3 non-profit youth basketball program in Juneau, Alaska. In 2007 I started freelancing and never looked back! My clients are amazing. I love working with them one-on-one; helping them build their business and understand how their vision translates on the web.

jules webb

I’m a front-end web developer. I use Adobe Photoshop, illustrator, and inDesign to create website layouts and print media. I work with HTML, CSS, SASS, Javascript, JQuery, PHP, MySQL, XML to create beautiful interactive Search Engine Optimized (SEO) websites. I develop with the Content Management Systems (CMS) Expression Engine or Wordpress. This gives my clients control over adding and editing their own content.

I Work With an Amazing Graphic Artist!

My name's Alvin Niwan and I’m a digital artist.

I live in Balikpapan, Indonesia and have created artwork for various businesses around the world. I’m tethered to my MACstation when hired, and my PLAYstation when vacant. I love to create from nothing, something great. I’m a fan of Dieter Rams and Jony Ive, but that doesn’t mean I always go with “less is more.”
